My CLoset is an android application that will allow users to manuever virtually through their closet.
My Closet has the ability to take all the different types of clothes in your closet, from tops, bottoms, to shoes, and make matches out of them in accordance with the occassion.
This means that our app will give you different suggestion matches, based off of whether you want to wear something athletic, formal, or even casual.
In order to use this app you would have to take pictures of all your clothing items in your closet, adding them to your camera roll.
After you download the app, you will have to create an account and from there you will be directed to our home page,
where you will be able to add items based off its color and style, then you pick the item from your camera roll and add to our inventory. Our app also creates a daily outfit for you, that gives you a randomly generated outfit you could wear day. We hope you love our app!
